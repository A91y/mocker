## Commerce
### Module to generate commerce and product related entries.

#### Output:

### price
Generates a price between min and max (inclusive).

|Option| Description|
|:------:|------|
|min|The minimum price.|
|max|The maximum price.|

#### Input:
|min|max|
|:----:|:----:|
|100|200|

#### Output:
114
